ABOUT US

Let's sensitize you on how to adopt good agricultural practices to better your yield

The National Tomato growers processors and marketers association of Nigeria is the umbrella body if all tomato value chain actors in Nigeria which comprises producers that is the farmers which include Large , medium and small holder farmers while processors include big and small scale processors and marketers include both male and female who engage them selfs in the tomato buying and selling wether fresh or processed .

WHAT WE OFFER

Services Rendered to our Members include but not limited to

Education

Farmers sensitizations on how to adopt Good Agricultural Practices for better yield in collaboration with all relevant agencies be it Government or non governmental organizations

Loan

Sentisatiins of our members on how to access loan from commercial banks to increase tomato production in the country.

Awareness

Due to the all year round emergence of some diseases they attack tomatoes like TUTA ABSOLUTA the Association is always together with farmers to record any emergence of any disease in order to curtail its spread within shortest possible time

Trade Association

The Association also is always trying to link our members with good business partners wether for buying farm imports or selling their produce after production

Secure Support

The Association also secures support from government and non Governmental organizations to its members always.

Project Management

The Association also try to collaborate with any project or program that can give support to our members like producers processors and Marketers within and out side the country

WHY CHOOSE US?

NATPAN aims to make Nigeria tomato sector more competitive and sustainable.

  • Improving Tomato productivity:
    NATPAN seek to address post harvest losses and promote efficient farming practices to increase tomato yields
  • Advocacy to support the policies:
    NATPAN push for the implementation of policies that protect local industries, provide incentives for farmers and promote partnership with international players for better seed quality.
  • Enhancing Market Access:
    NATPAN works to promote market access for Tomato farmers and processors ensuring Better income and sectoral stability
  • Promote Mechanization:
    Introduce technology such as tomato processing machines and solar dryers to reduce post harvest losses
  • Develop Infrastructure:
    Improve infrastructure for Tomato processing and storage
